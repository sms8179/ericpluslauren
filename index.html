Our Story

How We Met

We met at a house party during Sophomore year of college (I know, so romantic). Most of the patrons had left the party and Lauren was sitting on the couch waiting for her friend to be ready to leave. Eric walked over and asked her if she wanted to dance and she agreed. He walked her home and even let her wear his jacket (super ’50s style). We exchanged phone numbers and the rest is history!

The Proposal

Eric proposed to Lauren in front of one of her favorite place in New Orleans—in Jackson Square across from the Cathedral (Lauren loves the cathedral and imagined she’d get married there someday when she was little; Eric knew that she wanted to get married in Charlottesville to be close to his family and so he proposed to her there so they’d still have a memory at the cathedral). Lauren was COMPLETELY surprised—which is impressive considering pulling off a surprise on Lauren has never really worked in the past. We had another set of good friends with us so one of them snapped a picture. It was such an amazing moment captured—right when Eric knelt down—the shock on Lauren’s face is obvious. Then we all had beignets at Cafe du Monde and Champagne at the bar in the Hyatt French Quarter.
